Traveling China advices by Shan; Have fun )

Before landing, load your smart phone with some very useful apps.

  • Wechat - Must have messaging App in China; wechat is a de-facto communcation standard here
  • Pleco - English-Chinese Dictionary; pretty useful
  • Microsoft Translator - Translates English sentences to Chinese and vice-versa. (Make sure to download offline database for Simplified Chinese language)
  • Generally people don't speak English, so the Translator app can help you alot.
  • Baidu Maps - Help alot finding locations and buses/subways to get there.
  • Use the Translator app to translate names of places into chinese, then paste the name into Baidu Maps to get the location and transport information.

So you are all ready to explore the middle kingdom

  • Step1: Land China (Kinda neccessary for the all the followings hahahaa) :)
  • Buy a sim card at the airport (should cost around 150 RMB with 1GB Internet)
  • About Transport:
    • Get a transport card from a subway station. Charge it with 100-200 RMB or anything according to your use
    • Each bus trip here in Beijing costs between 1-2 RMB, and subway trip costs between 4-5 RMB.
    • Note: The transport card is different for different cities(As per my knowledge)
  • About money
    • The banks here use Unionpay system, but you can also use your VISA card(Don't know about other systems)
    • Wechat pay and alipay payments systems are very popular and convenient here, but if you're new, carry cash along.
  • ... Voila... Mission accomplished :)
