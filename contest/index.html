About Contest

Universal Programming Contest - For the universe.

The proBIT Programming Contest 2017 is the 1st instance of the annual universal programming contest series sponsored by BIT and Software Industry in Beijing, PRC.
The contest welcomes students from all the universities in Beijing. This year, the contest starts on March 25 2017 at 12:00 UTC. This is an open contest for students. Participants exclude the contest organisers, volunteers and members of the same group as the the contest chairs. Advance registration with an entry fee of 20 RMB per team is required. Contestants can register themselves as teams of atmost 2 persons. Any programming language(s) available on the contest system can be used to solve the problems and submit the solutions. There will be cash prizes and certificates for the first(winner) and second(runner-up) place teams. The first place team is “the discriminating hackers”, The team winning the judges’ prize is “a cool bunch of hackers”.
